Overview
--------
The GY-63 is a breakout board for the MS5611 pressure and temperature sensor.

Interface
---------
The GY-63 can communicate via I2C or SPI. We recommend using I2C as it requires less connections and it is easier to communicate with multiple sensors over I2C. The GY-63 operates at 5V.
